Corey McGrath

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in philosophy at the University of California, Santa Barbara.

My philosophical interests include philosophy of mind, metaphysics, and philosophical semantics. I am particularly interested in the philosophy of color, on which I am currently in the midst of completing my qualifying paper.

I also have side interests in early modern philosophy and philosophy of religion.

Before coming to UCSB, I earned a Bachelor of Arts in Philosophy at Geneseo State University in New York with minors in linguistics and anthropology. Afterwards I took graduate courses in philosophy and linguistics at the University of Buffalo, SUNY for two years.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I have worked in a number of industries, trying my hand at (among other things) marketing and web design. Currently, I maintain the UCSB Philosophy website and intern at Ocean o' Graphics. See my resume for more information.
