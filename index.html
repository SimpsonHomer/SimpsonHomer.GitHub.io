Using Logic 2010 for Philosophy 183

A user's guide for an educational logic program!

In the Spring of 2015 I was teaching assistant for Philosophy 183, Beginning Modern Logic. The software that the students were required to use for their homework assignments was Logic 2010, a useful program, but one with a steep learning curve and limited documentation. So, I wrote my own documentation, tailored for the needs of a Philosophy 183 student.
