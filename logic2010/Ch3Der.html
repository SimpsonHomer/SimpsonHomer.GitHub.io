Chapter 3 Problems

Derivations

We begin derivations as in Chapter 1. All derivations proceed as in Chapter 2, with the exception of a new form of derivation, Universal Derivation.

Universal Derivations

  1. Make a new line and enter 'Show Inst' in the center cell. The formula that occurs within the scope of the universal quantifier will appear on the line.
  2. Give a subderivation of the formula appropriate for the type of formula it is.
  3. Make a new line and enter the line number of the show line from Step 1 and the letters 'UD', in the center cell, separated by an empty space.
