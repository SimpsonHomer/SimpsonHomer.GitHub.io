Hi, welcome to my homepage. I am a third-year Ph.D. student in philosophy at the University of California, Santa Barbara.

My philosophical interests include philosophy of mind, metaphysics, and philosophical semantics. I am particularly interested in the philosophy of color, on which I am currently in the midst of completing my qualifying paper.I also have side interests in early modern philosophy and philosophy of religion.

Before coming to UCSB, I earned a Bachelor of Arts in Philosophy at Geneseo State University in New York with minors in linguistics and anthropology. Afterwards I took graduate courses in philosophy and linguistics at the University of Buffalo, SUNY for two years.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I have worked in a number of industries, trying my hand at (among other things) marketing and web design. Currently, I maintain the UCSB Philosophy website and intern at Ocean o' Graphics.

This site contains my academic and professional information, as well as resources for students. Click on 'about' for information about my research, as well as my CV and resume. If you're a student, you can find course materials and philosophy information on the resources page. Finally 'links' collects links to all sorts of pages.
